Sport Specific Parameters

A concise analysis of competitive youth parkour athletes

Testing Metrics

At the beginning and end of each season we asses sport specific metrics for athletic performance. For parkour, our best metrics are based on lower and upper body explosive strength as well as balance. Therefore, oure baseline and end of season testing is based on the standing broad jump and the dyno, which you can learn more about below.

  • Learn More

Testing Procedure

Standing broad jump is assessed using two raised blocks. Athletes need to jump brom one to the other and stick the landing without falling off. If successful, the distance between the closest edges of both boxes is measured and recorded. A dyno requires athletes to hang off of a ledge and pull themselves upwards to reach a point above the original ledge. Both hands must move together. The highest point reached by the lowest hand is the final measure.
